Frequently Asked Questions

Our customer service team is available Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM Eastern Time. While our online store is open 24/7, customer service inquiries received outside of business hours will be addressed on the next business day.

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. During peak periods or for complex inquiries, it may take up to 48 business hours to receive a comprehensive response. Rest assured, we're working diligently to address your questions as quickly as possible.

Currently, LeLabubu.ca operates exclusively as an online retailer with free shipping across montreal. While we don't have a physical storefront open to the public, we occasionally participate in pop-up events and collectible toy conventions in the Montreal area.
